    Help

  • Answers should begin with a 3 digit question number followed by a space and then your actual answer to the question.
  • Answers are case-insensitive and will only ever use letters, numbers, colons and hyphens. No other punctuation will be accepted.
  • When you submit a correct answer, you will see a confirmation and a new clue.
  • An answer is always incorrect if matched with a non-existent or incorrect question number.
  • The question numbers you receive will not be in order.
  • Correct answers need to be specific. There is some room for error (e.g. extra spaces are generally ignored) but not much.
  • If you're sure an answer is correct but it isn't working, double check everything. Worst case, you can ask Eric.
  • Some questions may have more than one correct answer. Sometimes different answers will give different results.
